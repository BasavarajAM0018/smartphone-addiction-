About This Application

Introduction

The Phone Addiction Tracker helps users understand their smartphone usage and spot early signs of dependence. By answering a short set of behavior- and emotion-based questions, you receive an easy-to-read % score, a risk category, common symptoms, and simple tips to improve balance.

Purpose

This app is designed to:

  • Increase awareness of digital habits.
  • Identify early signs of problematic phone use.
  • Provide actionable tips to reduce screen time.
  • Help students, professionals, parents and counselors monitor progress over time.

How it Works

The tool uses 18 short questions that cover daily routines, emotional triggers, and usage patterns. Responses are analyzed by:

  • A trained machine-learning model (if available) that computes a probability of addiction.
  • A rule-based fallback that converts the percentage of "Yes" answers into an addiction percentage.
  • Automatic handling for extremes: all "Yes" → 100% (Severe), all "No" → 0% (Low Risk).

Features

  • User registration and secure login
  • 18-question addiction test
  • Clear addiction percentage and category (Low / Mild / Moderate / Severe)
  • Tailored symptoms and practical tips for each category
  • Logs to review your past results
  • Simple, mobile-friendly interface

Who Can Use This?

This app is useful for students, working professionals, parents, teachers, counselors, and anyone who wants to manage screen time and build healthier phone habits.

Why This Matters

Excessive phone use can affect sleep, concentration, relationships, and mental well-being. Early recognition and small, consistent changes can significantly improve daily functioning.
